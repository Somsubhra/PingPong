How to play?

In this game, you just have to stop the ball from hitting the wall on your side by stroking the ball with the paddle to the opponents side. If the ball hits your opponenets wall you will score a point. The controls for the game are as follows:

  • Player 1 Up: W
  • Player 1 Down: S
  • Player 2 Up: Arrow Up
  • Player 2 Down: Arrow Down

The player with maximum points wins the game.
